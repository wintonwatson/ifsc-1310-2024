The University of Arkansas at Little Rock Research

Author: Christoper Bailey

UA little rock is a regional leader in using and teaching advanced technology in areas such as nanotechnology, information quality, and emerging analytics. The university is a leader in the perservation and dissemination of Arkansas history through the alignment of the department of history, the master of arts in public history program, and the Center of Arkansas History and Culture. Collections of 88,000 archival papers, images,and books, includng those of five Arkansas governors, provide a wealth of research opportunities.
